Key Aspects of Hacktoberfest

Goal:
The goal is to complete four pull requests (PRs) to eligible public repositories. Earn a limited-edition Hacktoberfest T-shirt or choose to plant a tree.

Participation Steps:
- Sign Up: Register on the Hacktoberfest website.
- Find Repositories: Look for repositories with the `hacktoberfest` label.
- Make Contributions: Submit valuable PRs.
- Track Progress: Follow your progress through the Hacktoberfest dashboard.

Types of Contributions:
- Bug fixes
- Documentation improvements
- Feature enhancements
- Tests or code refactoring
- New tools or projects

Community and Learning:
Hacktoberfest brings together a global community, offering participants a chance to learn, gain practical experience, and connect with open-source maintainers and developers worldwide.
